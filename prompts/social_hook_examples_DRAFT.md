## SENSITIVITY FLAGS (for agent to know what to avoid)
- **Gau rakshak**: Skip entirely — too religiously charged, brand risk too high
- **Godi media (B4)**: Use only if tone is very light — don't position as anti-BJP/pro-opposition
- **Kejriwal/Judge (B12)**: Never name the judge. Keep super generic. Consider skipping.
- **Operation Sindoor (A11)**: National pride only. Never reference attack/casualties.
- **NEET (B13)**: Empathetic tone required. Don't trivialize student pain.
- **AI Layoffs (B6)**: Acknowledge anxiety first, then pivot to stability — not "haha you got fired"
